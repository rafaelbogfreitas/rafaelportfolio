About Me

Hi, I'm Rafael. Hopefully I will give you an idea of myself in the next few lines. If there is one thing I can say about me, is that everytime I got interested in something I became an enthusiast. I like challenges and can spend hours, days or perhaps months trying to get a puzzle solved. I also love cycling, specially travelling on the bicycle. I have taken on the London to Paris route once and didn't enjoy the destination as much as I enjoyed the way to get there.

I currently work as floor manager in a busy London restaurant, where I have learned valuable life skills and work ethics. I am able to meet up with deadlines, to listen and accept critics, to adapt positively to changes and to work in a team.

Eight months ago I got introduced to web development and after trying out some HTML5 and seeing it displayed on the screen, I got addicted and couldn't stop learning as every new discovery led me to another one and so on. I then came up to the resolution that even though I really enjoy what I do now, I am passionate about web development and therefore want to do this for life.

Here you can see what I have achieved in my short journey in web development. Hope you enjoy!
